Igor Vakulenko

Biography

I became interested in electronics from a young age. My interests further grew once my family acquired a computer. Before I could even read, I used floppy disks to boot up and run software on the computer. Later, I taught myself how to use the Windows operating system and shortly after I was teaching my neighbours the basic use of a computer. During that time I also liked to disassemble toys and broken electronics to see how they worked.
